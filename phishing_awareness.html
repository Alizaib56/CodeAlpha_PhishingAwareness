🔐 Phishing Awareness Training

📌 What is Phishing?

Phishing is a cyber attack where attackers trick users into giving sensitive information like passwords or bank details.

⚠️ How to Identify Phishing Emails

  • Suspicious sender email
  • Urgent or threatening language
  • Fake links
  • Spelling mistakes

🌐 Fake Website Example

Check URL carefully: https://amaz0n-login.com ❌

Real: https://amazon.com ✅

🧠 Quiz

Is this phishing? "Your bank account is locked! Click here to verify."
