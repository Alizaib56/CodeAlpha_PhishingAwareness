📌 What is Phishing?

Phishing is a cyber attack where attackers trick users into revealing sensitive information such as passwords, credit card numbers, or personal data by pretending to be a trustworthy entity.

🔍 How to Identify Phishing

  • Check suspicious email addresses
  • Look for spelling or grammar mistakes
  • Avoid clicking unknown links
  • Verify website URLs carefully
  • Be cautious of urgent or threatening messages

⚠️ Fake Website Example

Example: www.paypa1.com (Fake)

Always verify domain names carefully. Attackers often use similar-looking characters.
