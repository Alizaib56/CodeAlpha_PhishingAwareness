🔐 Phishing Awareness Training

📌 What is Phishing?

Phishing is a cyberattack where attackers impersonate trusted organizations to steal sensitive data such as passwords, credit card details, or OTPs.

It commonly occurs via emails, fake websites, SMS, or calls.

⚠️ How to Recognize Phishing Emails

  • Fake or misspelled sender email
  • Urgent or threatening language
  • Suspicious links
  • Grammar mistakes
  • Unexpected attachments

🌐 Fake Website Detection

❌ Fake: https://amaz0n-login-secure.com

✅ Real: https://www.amazon.com

Always check HTTPS, domain spelling, and avoid extra words.

🧠 Social Engineering Tactics

  • Fear: "Your account is hacked!"
  • Urgency: "Act now!"
  • Authority: Pretending to be bank/government
  • Curiosity: "You received money!"

🛡️ Prevention Tips

  • Never click unknown links
  • Verify sender identity
  • Enable 2FA
  • Do not share OTPs
  • Use antivirus software
  • Keep system updated

🌍 Real-World Example

Email: "Your bank account is suspended. Click here to verify."

User clicks → enters credentials → attacker steals data.
